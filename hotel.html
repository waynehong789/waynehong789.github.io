There’s a lot of variety when it comes to Queenstown, New Zealand’s hotel and resort accommodation.

Many large hotels and resorts are located centrally within walking distance of the town's centre, and if you’re after something away from the hustle and bustle, hotels line the shores of Lake Wakatipu and can also be found in neighbouring Arrowtown.
